SPECIALIZE SPORTS PROGRAMMES FAQ

- Our school emphasizes a holistic approach, integrating academic excellence with athletic development. We have a flexible schedule that allows students to excel in both fields, with dedicated time for sports practices and academic study.
- While participation in sports is encouraged to foster teamwork, discipline, and fitness, students can choose from a range of athletic activities based on their interests and abilities. However, physical education is mandatory.
- We have a team of academic support staff, including tutors, who help students manage their workloads. Our school also provides dedicated study periods and flexible scheduling for students involved in sports competitions or practices.
- We offer a wide range of sports, including team sports (basketball, football, soccer, etc.), individual sports (tennis, athletics, swimming, etc.), and recreational activities to suit all interests and skill levels.
- We prioritize academic performance while also recognizing the importance of sports. If a student is involved in a competition, our faculty works with them to ensure they can catch up on missed lessons without affecting their grades.
- Yes, we have qualified coaches and trainers who work closely with students to develop their skills. We also provide opportunities for students to attend training camps and workshops with professionals in various sports fields.
- Absolutely! We encourage students to pursue sports both inside and outside of school. We support their participation in regional, national, or even international sports events.
- Teamwork is a core value in both our sports programs and classroom activities. We encourage collaborative projects in academics and emphasize the importance of supporting one another in sports.
- Our school provides a supportive environment where students can seek help from counselors, academic tutors, and coaches to find a balance. We also offer time management workshops to help students handle both responsibilities effectively.
- Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for students who excel in sports. These scholarships are available for both elite athletes and those showing great potential in their chosen sports.
- We have a wellness program in place that includes regular physical and mental health check-ups,stress management workshops, and balanced meal plans to support the well-being of students juggling both academics and sports.
- Yes, students are encouraged to compete at higher levels, and our flexible academic schedule is designed to accommodate such commitments. We work closely with students to ensure they don’t fall behind in their studies.
- Our dual focus on academics and sports ensures that students grow intellectually, physically, and socially. They develop discipline, time management, leadership skills, and resilience, which contribute to success in all aspects of life.
- While there are no strict academic prerequisites to join most sports teams, we do encourage students to maintain good academic standing. Athletes must demonstrate a commitment to both their academics and sports.
- Teachers and coaches communicate regularly to track students’ progress and well-being. We ensure that students have the resources they need to succeed in both academics and sports, and they are supported through consistent guidance from both faculty and coaching staff.
